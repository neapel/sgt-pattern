So. You have a by grid. By clicking (and dragging) with the left, middle, and right mouse button you can colour the cells black, uncertain, and white. The numbers in the margin tell you how many consecutive black cells there are in each row or column. Between two runs of black cells, there has to be at least on white cell. and colour all cells white or black.

Sweet.

Sorry but this game requires scripting and an HTML5 capable browser.
